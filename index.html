The architecture of time, revealed in its purest form.

Sapphire Double-domed sapphire crystal with multi-layer anti-reflective coating.

Heritage

A Legacy Reimagined for the Modern Horologist.

Born from a obsession with transparency and mechanical purity, the Equinox represents the pinnacle of our design philosophy. Each of the 244 components is finished by hand in our workshop, ensuring that what is visible is as beautiful as what is hidden.

Technical Excellence

Precision in Every Micron.

The Case

38mm round profile, crafted from Grade 5 titanium. Only 10mm thin with an integrated lug design that flows seamlessly into the wrist.

The Movement

In-house Caliber V.01 skeletonized manual-wind movement. 42-hour power reserve, 28,800 vph, and 21 jewels.

The Details

Slender orange seconds hand with a lume pip at its tip. Espresso leather strap with a custom titanium deployant clasp.
